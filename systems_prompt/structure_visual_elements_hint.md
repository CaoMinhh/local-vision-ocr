Preserve **document structure** and **reading order** (top-to-bottom, left-to-right where appropriate): keep heading levels, paragraphs, lists, and tables aligned with the layout; if the page has columns, sidebars, or boxed sections, reflect that order in your output (e.g. separate blocks or clear section breaks). Do not flatten everything into one undifferentiated paragraph when the source has clear structure.

Detect and **do not omit** non–plain-text or **visual** elements: **official stamps / seals** (e.g. round or rectangular agency seals), **signatures** (wet or scanned), **logos**, **letterheads**, **watermarks**, **rubber stamps**, **handwritten** notes or marginalia, and similar. **Transcribe** any legible text on them; if text is illegible or purely graphic, insert a **short bracketed description** at the logical position next to the related body text (e.g. `[Seal: circular red stamp, text illegible]`, `[Handwritten signature]`, `[Company logo: no readable text]`). Never silently skip these elements.
