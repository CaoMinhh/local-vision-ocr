If the text includes Vietnamese, the preamble should reasonably contain (in a sensible order): \documentclass{article}, \usepackage[T1]{fontenc}, \usepackage[utf8]{inputenc}, \usepackage[utf8]{vietnam}. Add packages when the content needs them: \usepackage{amsmath,amssymb} (math), \usepackage{array,booktabs} (tables), \usepackage{graphicx} (figures), \usepackage{hyperref} (links). Prefer a compilable document: full \documentclass, preamble, and \begin{document}…\end{document}.
